Hand Touch Instrument

How to Play:

Touch your fingertips together to trigger musical notes!

Thumb (tip) + Index (tip): C4 + D4
Thumb (tip) + Middle (tip): C4 + E4
Thumb (tip) + Ring (tip): C4 + F4
Thumb (tip) + Pinky (tip): C4 + G4

Tips:

  • Make sure your hand is visible in the camera frame
  • Try different finger combinations
  • Move your hand closer/further to adjust sensitivity
